Terms and conditions

1. Your Details

Applying for the loan involves submitting your details that will be used to identify you.
You are therefore required to provide the following:

1. Full names
2. ID number
3. Phone number

Your details are highly secured and you don't need to worry about it. Your data will never be used anywhere or by anyone.


2. Transaction fee

Before you receive your loan you MUST pay the transaction fee which is dependent on the amount of money you are applying for.

Your loan will be transferred IMMEDIATELY once your payment is confirmed at our office by the secretary' on duty.

3. Loan Repayment

The loan has NO interest rates - you pay the exact amount that you borrowed.

When you receive the loan, you will be required to pay it back within a minimum duration of 6 months and a maximum duration of 24 months.

A grace period of 6 months may be accepted only when the conditions are critical - in case you fail to pay the loan on time.
